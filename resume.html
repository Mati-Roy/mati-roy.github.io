Ought

I was contracted as an experiment participant for Ought in August 2019, but a month later was promotted to experiment runner, and I stayed in that role until the end of the experiments in January 2020.

My main responsibility was to manage a team of ~50 participants to test Factored Cognition — Ought's approach to answering open-ended questions. This included working on:

  • Onboarding participants
  • Tracking features and bugs
  • Creating experiments (writing scripts, coordinating participants, etc.)
  • Improving experiments (increasing speed, improving robustness, etc.)
  • Guiding the strategy development to stress test our incentive structure
  • Improving the incentive structure of our platform
  • Managing our communication channels, and organizing our documentation

Here’s more information on our research project: https://ought.org/presentations/delegating-cognitive-work-2019-06
